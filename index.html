Founded in Surat in 1969 by Mr. Sundarlal Shah and Mishrilal Shah, Suchi has continued to set benchmarks in the diamond cutting, polishing, and trading industry for over 50 years.

Mr. Nilesh, the second generation of the family in this business, moved from Surat to Mumbai to get first-hand experience of diamond trading. He spent days in the diamond manufacturing hub of India, creating loose parcels that were traded in the  market in Mumbai. He coined the brand name ‘Suchi’, by combining initials of the names of his cousins SUshma and CHIrag. 

Mr. Chirag, his brother,  joined the business 10 years into it and expanded the sales arena towards the North Indian Market and established our Delhi Headquarters and a branch in Ludhiana which is our central office for the state of Punjab.

Miss Niti, the third generation of the family in this business, has now added onto the brand legacy by establishing a new vertical in the Family Business. 

She is at the forefront of efforts to take Suchi on a path of growth while maintaining the traditional tenets of quality and trust that the brand has gained over the years. She recently established Suchi Jewels,  the wholesale house of high diamond jewellery.
